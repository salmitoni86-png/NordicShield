# Nordic Shield Development Roadmap

## Milestone 1 - Foundation

Goal:
Create a playable strategic map.

Tasks:

- Nordic region map
- Zoom controls
- Unit markers
- Pathfinding
- Replay recording

## Milestone 2 - Warfare

Goal:
First combat-capable build.

Tasks:

- Ground combat
- Air combat
- Missile systems
- Detection systems

## Milestone 3 - Strategy

Goal:
Create strategic depth.

Tasks:

- Logistics
- Supply lines
- Fuel system
- Economy

## Milestone 4 - Campaign

Goal:
Story mode.

Tasks:

- Dynamic events
- Political decisions
- Branching outcomes

## Milestone 5 - Multiplayer

Goal:
Online warfare.

Tasks:

- Dedicated servers
- Matchmaking
- Shared campaigns
